The Social Problem

We all know how COVID-19 is spreading and affecting people. Lot of measures have been taken by individuals and the government of India which includes lock down of the country. During the lockdown, many people who stay in slums, migrants, and daily wage earners are getting affected the most and are most vulnerable to COVID-19.

The Social Solution

Zaitoon foundation team has taken the initiative to distribute Food, Clothing and Grocery to the needy people. Upliftment of Socially Backward People. Prioritizing supply of Essential Items to the one 's who need. This distribution drive will take place in India Pune Australia Sydney once enough funds are raised
